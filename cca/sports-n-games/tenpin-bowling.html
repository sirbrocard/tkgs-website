Tenpin Bowling

Tenpin bowling has a rich, memorable history that spans over many years. It is a sport that requires physical and mental strength, team spirit, and good sportsmanship. It also helps to build one’s confidence. Under the training of our experienced coach, we have consistently emerged as one of the top 4 schools in the National inter-schools Bowling Competition. All training sessions are conducted at external bowling centres. While the CCA is supported by school funds, addition coaching and lane fees and equipment have to be paid for by the students. The school will provide one-way transport to training venues.
