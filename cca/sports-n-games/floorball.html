Floorball

Floorball was introduced in TKGS in 2015. We have lofty ambitions in becoming one of the top schools in Singapore for Floorball. We know that success won’t come easy. It takes commitment and resilience to achieve excellence. So if you have a strong interest in Floorball and are ready to commit yourself to the game, we welcome you to join us. We will be pioneers in the journey together to build Floorball into a CCA of excellence.

Nationals 'C' Division 87th Position
