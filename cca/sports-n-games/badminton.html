Badminton

Badminton is a sport which demands hard work, perseverance, commitment and determination. We are trained by two very dedicated and experienced coaches – Mrs Juliana Tan (ex-player under combined schools squad) and Mr Wong Shoon Keat who was an ex-national player. Most of the team players started from scratch, without any knowledge of the game. Therefore in the first year, training is focused mainly on grasping the basics and rules of the game.

The seniors in the B Division help to train the juniors, and that foster close relationships between members of both teams. The B Division team also honed their skills with teams from Xinmin Secondary School and Victoria Junior College. The B Division team has consistently been placed top 4 amongst the East Zone school teams.
