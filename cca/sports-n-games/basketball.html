Basketball

We are a group of players united by our love and devotion to the game of Basketball. We train hard and play hard, with our love for the sport and mutual respect for each other as teammates spurring us on.

With disciplined and consistent training, our players acquire expertise in basketball quickly, even though not all of us may have had prior experience in the game. As such, no ‘newbie’ gets left behind as we encourage each other towards improving ourselves as a team. To further enhance our skills, we participate in as many competitions and games as possible. Our coach and teachers make conscious efforts to maximise the exposure that each of us can experience through competitions or tournaments.

If you are game about Basketball, and wish to be given an equal chance to play in major tournaments, we warmly invite you to join us. We will groom and nurture you to be the best Basketballer you can be!
