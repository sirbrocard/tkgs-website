Tennis

Tennis is a sport that requires not only discipline, commitment and determination but also self-leadership. A good Tennis player constantly reflects on his/her performance while learning how to respond with tenacity and agility to unexpected conditions in the moment. A tennis player must have the mental strength to overcome a pressurizing match situation - as well as the ability to understand the opponents’ strengths and weaknesses in order to gain the upper hand in the game. Players also learn the values of humility and graciousness in times of glory and setbacks. Above all, they learn to ride on the value of integrity at all times.

If you have the passion for Tennis and are ever ready to work hard to bring glory to TKGS, make Tennis your choice CCA!
