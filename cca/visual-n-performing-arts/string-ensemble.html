String Ensemble

Established in 1998, the String Ensemble has grown steadily, from a very modest 10-member team to the current 40-strong ensemble.

The ensemble has participated regularly in the biennial Singapore Youth Festival, clinching gold and silver awards. In the recent Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Arts Festivals, the String Ensemble was awarded the highest accolade, the Certificate of Distinction.

Dedicated to providing our young musicians with musical, social and intellectual growth, the string ensemble has nurtured many music enthusiasts and young talents and will continue to do so for years to come.
