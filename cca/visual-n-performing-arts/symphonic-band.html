2017

Exchange with Edina High School

On 27 March 2017, TKGS gave a wonderfully warm welcome to our American visitors from Edina High School Band, Minnesota. After introductions by student leaders and teachers, joint sectionals started in full swing.

Learn more

It was a privilege seeing the way music has the unique ability to unite people from across the globe. It was great to see the students coming together with a common love for great music. The renditions of local and American pieces played by both bands showcased our strong ties of friendship.Hands to do our Part in Marine Conservation”.

Since the beginning of August, the Art Clubbers and members from the Green Auditors collaborated by working together in planning out their ideas for the proposals. They eventually created the sculpture when one of the teams’ proposals was shortlisted. The Green Auditors helped garner the necessary materials while the Art Clubbers helped to conceptualise the artistic qualities of the sculpture. Using recyclable materials they had gathered, they made sculptures of all kinds of marine creatures - from corals to penguins and turtles.

The joint concert between Edina High School and TKGS Symphonic Band on the evening of 28 March at the Esplanade Concert Hall ended on a high note. The combined band performed Jan Van de Roost’s “Singapura Suite” and Karl King’s “Rough Riders” were specially chosen to represent the cultural identity of each country.On 27 March 2017, TKGS gave a wonderfully warm welcome to our American visitors from Edina High School Band, Minnesota. 2018

Limelight at Esplanade

TKGS Symphonic Band held their limelight at the Esplanade. They had an extensive repertoire consisting of the Singapore Youth Festival set piece “Sunny Island” by local composer Wong Kah Chun, and choice piece “In the Persian Market” by Albert William Ketelbey.

It was the first performance for the Sec 2 members. Despite the nervousness, the band played well and was applauded upon the finale of their performance. Once again, TKGS Symphonic Band continues to uphold their track record of excellence in music!
