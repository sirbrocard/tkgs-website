First Day of School for Sec 1 Students

We would love to see our Secondary One TKGians report to school on the first day of school in their full school uniform as it is an important part of school identity. However, in view of the safe management measures implemented to contain the spread of COVID-19, we understand that parents may require a longer time to purchase the school uniform and PE attire. As such, your daughter may wear her primary school attire for the month of January.

From 5 Jan onwards, your daughter may wish to report to school in her PE t-shirt (instead of school blouse) with the TKGS pinafore/primary school uniform. This provision for TKGians to wear the PE t-shirt (instead of the school blouse) will be in place until mask-wearing is no longer mandatory.

Lessons will commence on 8 Jan. The student can bring her learning materials for the day according to the timetable, which will be issued to her during the first week of school. The student could inform her teachers if she needs more time to purchase the learning materials.
Singapore Citizens in financial need can get assistance from MOE on school fees and other expenses from: https://tkgs.moe.edu.sg/qql/slot/u424/MOE%20FAS%20Application%20Form%202021.pdf
